What is Pair Programming?

When new friends program together.

Why is Pair Programming Important

Being able to proofread your partner's work.

Compare Navigator and Driver

The navigator and driver both discuss what the strategy will be. The driver types the code while the navigator directs them and catches any mistakes.

Reflection

This Was my first GPS and it ultimately went well! Having a guided pairing session is a little disconcerting at first; after all, any time you're evaluated in real time, in addition to meeting your partner for the first time, is going to have a different dynamic, and its impossible to not feel any kind of pressure. Still, in the hour (we scheduled the GPS for 12 am, so time was an issue) we were in the GPS, we managed to produce a page that, while consciously sparse, still demonstrates a competency with basic HTML and CSS.
